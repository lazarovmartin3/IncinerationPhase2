Please use the sampe scene.
When the scene is loaded and run, click left mouse button to spawn the spheres. 
They will start in a random velocity and direction.

In the editor, the script Spawner is attached to Playground object. In the inspector, you can find
variables which you can modify. 
Number of spheres determine how many spheres will be spawned in the scene.
Once clicked, you cannot respawn more spheres. 
Boundaries variable determine the playground area. If the Draw Gizmos is active, you can
see the boundaries on the screen. Which will be easier to determine the playground.
The spheres will move inside the boundaries. On coliision with eachother, they will change 
its color to red.
